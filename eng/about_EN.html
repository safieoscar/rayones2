About Us

Rayones de El Salvador

Our History

Rayones de EL Salvador was born with our founder Don Abraham Safie more than 54 years ago, in order to make El Salvador an industrialized country. It is currently one of the best and most modernly equipped textile factories in the Central American Region. Located in an industrial zone of El Salvador, the administrative offices and the production plant are located in the city of Ilopango, San Salvador, a few blocks from the Airport.

Social Responsability

Mission

Produce yarns and fabrics of the highest quality, with a highly qualified staff and equipment, which allows us to provide our customers with the best and widest variety of textile products, with timely deliveries and excellent quality.
